Our Story

A kitchen built around fire, not fuss

Olive & Ember began as a single wood-fired hearth in the back of a neighborhood wine bar. What started as a weekend supper club for regulars grew into a full dining room, but the philosophy never changed: cook honestly, source locally, and let a live flame do the work a saucepan can't. Every plate that leaves our kitchen still passes over the same embers we started with.
